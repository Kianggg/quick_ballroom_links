Naming convention for gif lookup:

1wB01


First char:
1 - Smooth
2 - Standard
3 - Rhythm
4 - Latin

Second char:
w - Waltz
t - Tango
f - Foxtrot
v - Viennese
q - Quickstep

c - Cha-cha
r - Rumba
i - Swing *** this one is weird to avoid conflict with samba! ***
b - Bolero
m - Mambo

s - Samba
p - Paso Doble
j - Jive

Third char:
B - Bronze
S - Silver
G - Gold

Fourth and Fifth chars:
the number figure it is in the given syllabus
